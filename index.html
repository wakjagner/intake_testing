    Apply for a permit with Traffic Control

    This permit is required for the temporary use or closure of a sidewalk, bike/traffic lane, or street, and any associated parking.
    **Sidewalk use: Using a portion of the sidewalk while maintaining pedestrian access.


    Application Checklist:
    If your work involves the use or closure of a sidewalk, bike/traffic lane, and/or street, you must submit the following documents:

    A Traffic Control Plan (TCP) showing how your work affects traffic at the jobsite (pedestrian or vehicle) for traffic engineer review. You can attach your TCP in the "TRAFFIC CONTROL PLAN or WORK SPACE DIAGRAM" section below. For more information on what an appropriate TCP looks like, please visit our Traffic Control Plan Information Page.
    In some situations, this can be a Work Space Diagram showing your planned area of impact to the right of way. Indicate this area by drawing a box or other polygon on the PBOT BaseMap. Here is an example of a simple work space diagram: Work Space Diagram. Please Click Here for help deciding if a site specific traffic control plan is needed.
